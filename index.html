Quick Glance

red panda is an animal that currently resides in the eastern himalayas. It is currently an endangered species with a population of less than 10,000 indiviuals. The animal does not look very similar to its commonly known relative the "Panda", it however is different being in size since it is significiantly more smaller. The Red Panda is just a bit bigger than your normal domestic housecat, and the Red Panda is more acrobatic than the Panda. The color of its fur contains a reddish brownish tint giving it the name "Red Panda".

Fact Check

  • The Scientific name for the Red Panda is "Aliurus Fulgens"
  • Red Pandas are Herbivores
  • Red Pandas spend 50% of their time in trees
  • Red Pandas are typically killed by traps meant for other animals like bear
  • Red Panda caps and hats have been made out of the animal and sold.
